From Searching to Finding Peace

Shared by the Creative Calling team

For years, I searched for meaning in all the wrong places — in achievements, in others' approval, in spiritual practices that promised power and enlightenment. Each path left me more empty than before.

One quiet evening, exhausted from striving, I simply prayed: "God, if You're real, please show me." What followed wasn't dramatic — no lightning, no visions. Just a gentle sense of being known, loved, and invited home.

Through reading Scripture and learning about Jesus, I discovered that what I'd been searching for had been searching for me all along. The God who created the universe cared about my restless heart. The peace I'd been chasing was found in simply resting in His presence.

"Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest." — Matthew 11:28
